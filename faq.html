Yes. Every Cloud Form cover is fully removable, secured with heavy-duty velcro. We recommend professional dry cleaning only.

You can also order a replacement cover at any time — same fabric, different fabric or different colour. The cover is designed to be replaced when you want a refresh, not when you have to.

For cotton and corduroy: vacuum regularly and spot-clean with a gentle fabric cleaner. Send the cover to a professional dry cleaner every 6-12 months depending on use.

For leather: condition every 6 months with a leather-specific product to keep the surface supple.

Avoid prolonged direct sunlight on any fabric — it shifts colour over time.

7-day returns from the date of delivery for verified manufacturing defects. We provide a 1-year warranty covering structural and craftsmanship issues — frame, stitching, connectors.

The warranty does not cover fabric stains, scratches or mechanical damage caused by use. If something happens to the cover, you can order a replacement at any time.

We don't recommend Cloud Form for homes with pets. The fabrics are durable, but accidents like urine, scratching or chewing fall outside the warranty and the cushioning isn't designed to recover from that kind of damage. If you still want one and have pets, message us — we'll be honest about what to expect.
